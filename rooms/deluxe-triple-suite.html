Room Overview

The deluxe triple suite is spacious, stylishly decorated and features a double bed as well as a sofa bed which can accommodate up to three people. The room also features a spacious closet, mini-fridge, two modern bathrooms, and all the necessary amenities that will make you feel right at home. Lastly, the room has direct access to a furnished balcony with sea and mountain view.
