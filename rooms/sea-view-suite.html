Sea View Suite

Premium suite with partial sea view and private balcony. Spacious layout and elegant details for an unforgettable stay in Thassos.

Room Overview

The Sea View Suite combines comfort and style with a generous interior and a private balcony where you can enjoy Thassos sunsets. Ideal for couples or small families looking for extra space and a touch of luxury.

The suite includes a modern bathroom, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, Wi‑Fi and daily housekeeping.
