LIMENARIA

LAFEYRA HOTEL

LaFeyra Hotel is situated in the picturesque village of Limenaria, on the southern part of Thassos island. Conveniently located, just a few meters away from the wonderful beach of Limenaria, yet sufficiently close to the center of the village with all the taverns, restaurants, shops and bars. The breathing distance from the beach gives you the opportunity to spend quality and relaxing time enjoying the crystal clear waters of the Aegean sea, without moving far from the comfort of your hotel. LaFeyra Hotel is an ideal choice for anyone who wishes to experience the beauties of Thassos island, by enjoying a relaxed holiday at the same time.

THASSOS

THE EMERALD ISLAND

Thassos island is the most northerly of all the Greek islands and can be found in the Aegean Sea nestled between the Halkidiki Peninsular and Kavala. Conveniently located, it is only a few kilometres away from the nearest ports on the mainland.
